A little about Brandenburg

Brandenburg is one of the 16 federal states of Germany. Before unification it belonged to the German Democratic Republic.

Brandenburg has had an environmental protection policy since the early 1990s. It has 15 protected areas made up of a National Park, Biosphere Reserves and Natural Parks.

Potsdam, the capital, is known for hosting the former residences of the Prussian kings. It has a large number of palaces and parks whose natural landscapes are part of the Cultural and Natural Heritage of Unesco.
