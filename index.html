Casual traveler discovering Brandenburg

Why do I want to show everything I discovered in Brandenburg?

I feel that it is an unknown state because it belonged to the German Democratic Republic, it has a lot of potential and unfortunately it does not have the economic development of the other current German states.

On the other hand, his anonymity allows him to preserve natural and cultural spaces in an optimal way.

The Germans are generally unaware of the potential of this region. Although little by little, the Berliners are becoming more present in it trying to escape the stress that the city proposes.

I feel that it is an unknown land that preserves its originality thanks to the ignorance or prejudice that the Germans have about this state. That is why through this medium I try to make visible what is hidden in this territory.

National parks, forests and lagoons. A landscape that transmits Peace

This state has a large number of lakes which are suitable for recreational use. Both artificial and natural forests give the region a rugged landscape full of clean air.

I find it fascinating that in just 45 minutes from Berlin one can escape from the city and meet nature. These are contrasts that I don't know if they occur in other regions.

Potsdam. The capital and its palaces

Probably, they have not known about this neighboring city of Berlin but in it are the old palaces of the German kings.

A gigantic park of thousands of hectares houses palaces, gardens and forests planned by the kings and nobility of their time. A key point when visiting Berlin and its surroundings.
